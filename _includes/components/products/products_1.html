Our main focus is creating an integrated solution to tackle every aspect of the practical problem. Mobile applications for easy on the go control over the situation and a desktop/web solution providing detailed view and a comprehensive management possibilities.



Managing the operations of a laboratory with a Lab4U integrated solution

Lab4U, integrated platform manages all aspects of the laboratory processes, regardless of the sample source, complexity, and different parameters of the process analysis.


Features:

  • Omni-channel approach to sample analysis

  • Starting the analysis process on the go by scanning or entering relevant sample data with mobile app.

  • Desktop application for employees who work in the process on site and require a detailed overview and complete information about the state of the process and parameters of the analysis.

  • Real-time notifications and escalations for efficient sample management.

  • Workload management module, multi-level lab entry books, customisation via administration module and more...
